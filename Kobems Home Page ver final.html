About Us

We are a rescue shelter and a pet adoption website. Our goal is to save as many animals as humanly possible, to give them a forever home! We at golden paws hold adoption events to help raise funds and help animals to get adopted. Our thought process for golden paws was “how can we help dogs” and so we made a shelter and a website to spread our viewpoint on saving every single dog humanly possible.
