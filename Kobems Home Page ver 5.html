About Us

We are a rescue shelter and a pet adoption website. Our goal is to save as many animals as humanly possible, to give them a forever home! We at golden paws hold adoption events to help raise funds and help animals to get adopted. Our thought process for golden paws was “how can we help dogs” and so we made a shelter and a website to spread our viewpoint on saving every single dog humanly possible.

More Information about us

The Golden Paws Company from Kobems was formed with animal safety and protection in mind. This is largely due to the rising euthanization of dogs in kill shelters all around America. Kobems as a company founded Golden Paws to answer the question: “what can we do to help bring the average euthanization of dogs down?” We saw a chance to make a shelter-based website to bring down dog euthanization. With this in mind we made our website to showcase the dogs in kill shelters and help them find homes. We’re looking forward to adoption events and collaborating with companies in the future.
